About The Aegis Project

Our Mission

To build a future where facial recognition technology is safe, fair, and accountable for all. Our mission is to develop and deploy cutting-edge AI solutions that uphold privacy, eliminate bias, and empower users with full transparency and control over their data.

Why "Aegis"?

In mythology, the Aegis was the shield of the gods, a symbol of divine protection. We chose this name because Aegis is designed to be a protective framework, shielding individuals from the potential misuse of facial recognition technology while fostering trust and security in its application.
